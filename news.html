8 February 2026

Young Women Climate Mentorship Programme Closes First Cohort

We proudly closed the first cohort of the Young Women Climate Mentorship Programme with a celebration of leadership, learning, and youth-led climate action. Twenty young women completed six months of mentorship, training and field visits, presenting four community-based climate projects at the closing ceremony.

Mentorship
13 June 2025

YICA, YCC and Partners Complete Youth NDC Consultations in Bo and Makeni

Youth Initiative for Climate Action and the Youth Climate Council completed the first two stops of the national Youth NDC Consultation tour, bringing sixty young leaders into direct conversation about Sierra Leone's climate commitments in Bo and Makeni.

Policy
14 April 2025

YICA Leads Two-Day NDC Consultation for Young Women and Girls

YICA facilitated a two-day Nationally Determined Contributions workshop specifically for young women and girls across Sierra Leone, collecting input on adaptation, mitigation and gender equality for submission to key stakeholders.

Policy
14 December 2024

Launch of the Youth Climate Council Freetown Chapter

YICA-SL launched the Youth Climate Council Freetown Chapter at the Freetown City Council Hall, marking the first local chapter of YCC Sierra Leone. Mayor Yvonne Aki-Sawyerr co-signed the launch, emphasising youth leadership in local climate action.

Launch
1 August 2024

Youth Climate Council Sierra Leone Officially Launched

YICA officially launched the Youth Climate Council Sierra Leone on the theme "Bridging Intergeneration Gap for Equitable Climate Action & Policy Formulation," bringing together key stakeholders including the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change.
