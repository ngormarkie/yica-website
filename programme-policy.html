Policy Engagement

National Policy Engagement

Bridging community-level concerns with national decision-making — so young people's priorities shape Sierra Leone's environmental and climate policy.

Since 2022, YICA-SL has maintained active engagement with national government agencies to ensure youth perspectives are included in environmental and climate policy.

Shaping Sierra Leone's NDCs

A significant achievement of our policy work has been facilitating youth consultations that informed Sierra Leone's updated Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) — the country's climate commitments to the international community. Working with the Youth Climate Council Sierra Leone, we organised consultations across all regions and developed a comprehensive youth position paper.

  • Organised youth forums to gather input on climate priorities
  • Synthesised youth recommendations for submission to the NDC process
  • Advocated for ambitious targets that address youth concerns

Community-level advocacy

Beyond national policy, YICA-SL strengthens environmental governance locally by organising community dialogues with local councils, documenting environmental violations and presenting findings to authorities, and supporting community representatives to participate in chiefdom and district planning.
